About Me

Hello. I'm Marco, and I'm a junior full-stack developer currently living in New York City. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive (WDI) where I not only learned fundamental back and front-end languages, but also important programming methodologies and best-practices.

I've always been a creative type and loved building stuff. Legos were my thing when I was a kid! That turned into the desire to become an architect when I grew up. As a result, I developed an appreciation for good design, form and user experience. Now I'm an architect of online programs!

Before becoming a web developer, I managed and cultivated the online brands for two startups using primarily social media channels and email marketing. I also coordinated events for tech professionals and entrepreneurs in New York. I got my first taste of development while updating online content. I had taken a peek behind the 'magic' veil of a website and was intrigued. That itch to know more about the code led me to learn how to do it for myself.
