About Me

Hello

I'm Marco, and I'm a junior full-stack developer currently living in New York City. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive (WDI) where I not only learned fundamental back and front-end languages, but also important programming methodologies and best-practices.

I've always been a creative type and love building stuff. Legos were my thing as a kid! That turned into the desire to become an architect when I grew up. As a result, I developed an appreciation for good design, form and user experience. Now I'm an architect of online programs!

Before becoming a web developer, I managed and cultivated the online brands for two startups using primarily social media channels and email marketing. I also coordinated events for tech professionals and entrepreneurs in New York. My first taste of development was while updating online content and got the see the raw code. I had taken a peek behind the 'magic' veil of a website and was intrigued. That itch to know more about the code is what eventually led me to learn how to do it for myself.
