About

Hello

I'm Marco, a Front-End Web Developer living in New York City. I have been working as a Software Engineer at Sprinklr since 2014, building client-facing web applications for enterprise companies. I started my journey into web development after I graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive (WDI) program earlier in 2014, where I not only learned fundamental front- and back-end languages, but also important programming methodologies and best-practices.

I am a creative individual, and I love building things. As a child, I used to LOVE Legos! This exposure turned into the desire to become an architect when I grew up. As a result, I developed an appreciation for good design, form and user experience. Now I can architect complete web applications!

Before becoming a web developer, I managed and cultivated the online brands for two startups using primarily social media channels and email marketing. I also coordinated events for tech professionals and entrepreneurs in New York. I was introduced to development while updating online content and took a peek behind the 'magic' veil of a website, and tinkered with the HTML and CSS. I was hooked. That itch to know more about the code is what eventually led me to where I am today.
