    About Me

    Joe Redman grew up in Northwestern Indiana and trained in Opera, Musical Theatre, and Clarinet at Ball State University (2011). During his senior year of college, he auditioned for Disney and went on to perform for Mickey Mouse over the next three years at Disney World, Disney Cruise Line, and Tokyo Disneyland. Joe’s has been seen in: The Runaway Clone (Crown Theatre, Directed by Mia Sommese), The Full Monty (Music Directed by Jeff Caldwell), Angels in America:

    Millennium Approaches (Ball State University – Directed by Jennifer Blackmer), South Pacific (Gaslight Theatre Co. – Directed by Christianne Chase), My Fair Lady (Ball State University – Directed by Michael O’Hara), and many more! Joe lives in New York City and has been securing his own work consistently since graduating from college. He is interested in many forms of performance and is currently looking for an agent.
